Team Structure

At Pacific Union we know that best Loan Officers need to know they have the support of the best possible team and that’s why our loan officers work with the same team each time. We’re differentiated by our operations team structure – specifically the Sales Coordinator role. The This structure creates consistency and scalability through the formation of fulfillment teams at each operations center, with each team engineered to support an average of 170 units per month including several roles:

  • Sales Coordinator – Answers loan scenario questions, prioritizes and manages loan pipelines, and serves as the single point of contact for the loan officer and their loan related questions
  • Processor – Prepares files for underwriting, clears conditions, and coordinates closing schedules
  • Underwriter – Evaluates risk, verifies adherence to guidelines, helps to clear conditions, and communicates decisions and conditions to the Loan Officer & Processor
  • Doc Draw/Closer/Funder – Manages final funding conditions, coordinates funding schedules, prepares closing disclosures, and authorizes release of funds

A personal solution to an industry problem. That’s the Pacific Union way.
