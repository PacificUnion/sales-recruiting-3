Team Structure

At Pacific Union, we know Loan Officers need the support of the best possible team. That’s why we built our operations team around the Sales Coordinator role. At Pacific Union, Loan Officers work with the same dedicated team for each loan, made up of in-branch Processor, in-branch Loan Officer Assistant, Sales Coordinator, Underwriter, and Closer.

Sales Coordinator
Non-Production role strictly focused on ensuring communication of file status updates, pipeline management, product scenarios and overall streamlined process.

✔ 100% Focused on customer service to sales teams
✔ Managing the pipeline

  • By lock expiration, estimated closing date, volume, cycle time, disclosure rejection list, conditions, and more
  • Pipeline compliance, movement, and cleaning

✔ Sales Training

  • Welcome calls & product scenarios

✔ Proactive Communication

  • Timely feedback on file status
  • Weekly calls with sales for pipeline review, identifying upcoming client visits, confirming delivery and funding goals, and scheduling welcome calls

A personal solution to an industry problem. That’s the Pacific Union way.

Technology

Our CRM is integrated with the LOS so marketing campaigns are automatically triggered.

  • In-Process Email Campaign – this is a series of video emails sent as various milestones are reached
  • Post-Close Follow-Up Campaign – each borrower is added to this 5-year campaign after they have closed their loan, including an address stamper gift
  • Turnkey emails – monthly emails sent to your contact database on your behalf
  • Markets in a Minute – weekly email sent to business partners regarding the housing and financial markets

Our LOS creates easy and transparent communication.

  • Includes all functions needed to take a loan application and submit to processing, with real-time pricing
  • Data Validation software included for Automated Underwriting
  • All documents generated can be found on a single screen, including Underwriting Approval letters
  • Built in closing fee templates save time and help to ensure accurate disclosures

Marketing

Loan Officers need to set themselves apart. That’s why we built a hand-picked marketing team with expertise in everything from social media to print ads to email campaigns – to make sure our people don’t get lost in the noise.

Digital Marketing & Video

We eat, sleep, and breathe social media and videography. So when we create images and posts for sharing on LinkedIn and Facebook, you’re getting optimized content. But it doesn’t end there. Print Collateral
